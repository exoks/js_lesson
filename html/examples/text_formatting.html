=== <b> & <strong> ===
    <b> is used for styling <b>Word</b> == Word
    <strong> is used to show emphasis <strong>Word</strong> == Word

    
    === <u> & <ins> ===
    <u> is used for styling <u>Word</u> === Word
    <ins> is used for inserted text <ins>Word</ins> === Word

    
    === <s> & <del> ===
    <s> (strikethrought) is used for styling <s>Word</s> === Word
    <del> is used to display that text is deleted <del>Word</del> === Word

    
    === <mark> ===
    <mark> is used to highlight the text <mark>Word<mark> === Word

    
    === <sup> & <sub> ===
    <sup> is used to display a small text above 2<sup>2</sup> === 22
    <sub> is used to display a small text bellow O<sub>2</sub> === O2
